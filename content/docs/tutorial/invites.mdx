## Common Mistakes to Avoid

<div className="space-y-4 my-8">
  <Callout type="error" title="Spamming Referral Links">
    Don't spam your referral link everywhere. This can get you banned from communities and platforms. Focus on providing value and sharing your link organically.
  </Callout>

  <Callout type="error" title="Ignoring Quality Over Quantity">
    Don't just focus on getting as many sign-ups as possible. One active trader is worth more than 100 inactive accounts. Focus on attracting genuine users.
  </Callout>

  <Callout type="error" title="Not Tracking Performance">
    Failing to track which channels and strategies work best means you're wasting effort. Use analytics to optimize your approach and focus on what works.
  </Callout>

  <Callout type="error" title="Forgetting to Withdraw">
    Don't let your commissions sit idle. Withdraw regularly (above the $50 minimum) to secure your earnings and reinvest or use them as you wish.
  </Callout>

  <Callout type="error" title="Violating Platform Rules">
    Never create fake accounts or engage in fraudulent activity to boost referrals. This will result in account termination and loss of all earnings.
  </Callout>
</div>
