## Common Questions

<Tabs items={['Can I negotiate minimums?', 'What if I want to invest more?', 'Are there hidden fees?', 'Can minimums change?']}>
<Tab value="Can I negotiate minimums?">

### Can I Negotiate Minimum Investment Amounts?

Generally, minimum investment amounts are **non-negotiable** for retail investors, as they're set by the startup and approved by regulatory frameworks.

**However, exceptions may exist for:**
- Institutional investors or family offices
- Strategic investors with industry expertise
- Lead investors willing to coordinate the funding round
- Special circumstances approved on a case-by-case basis

<Callout type="info">
  If you believe you have a unique situation, contact NYYU support to discuss potential options with the startup.
</Callout>

</Tab>
<Tab value="What if I want to invest more?">

### Investing Above the Minimum

You can invest **any amount above the minimum**, up to the stated maximum per investor:

1. **Review the maximum cap** on the startup's investment page
2. **Ensure your wallet has sufficient funds** for the desired amount
3. **Enter your investment amount** during the checkout process
4. **Complete the transaction** as normal

**Benefits of larger investments:**
- Larger equity stake in the company
- Potentially better terms or bonuses
- Stronger relationship with founders
- More significant returns if the startup succeeds

</Tab>
<Tab value="Are there hidden fees?">

### Fee Transparency

NYYU maintains **complete transparency** regarding all fees:

- **Platform Fee**: Clearly disclosed before investment (typically 2-5%)
- **Payment Processing**: Standard payment gateway fees if applicable
- **No Hidden Charges**: What you see is what you pay

All fees are shown during the investment checkout process before you confirm the transaction.

<Callout type="tip">
  Review the "Investment Summary" screen carefully before confirming to understand all fees and your final equity stake.
</Callout>

</Tab>
<Tab value="Can minimums change?">

### Do Minimum Requirements Change?

Minimum investment amounts can change during a funding round:

**Reasons for changes:**
- **High demand**: Minimums may increase if the round is oversubscribed
- **Low demand**: Minimums may decrease to attract more investors
- **Time-based**: Early bird specials may offer lower minimums initially
- **Regulatory updates**: Legal requirements may necessitate changes

**How you're protected:**
- The minimum shown when you **commit** to invest is locked for your transaction
- You'll be notified if minimums change for future investments
- Completed investments are never affected by subsequent changes

</Tab>
</Tabs>
