## Common Issues & Solutions

<Tabs items={['Wallet Insufficient Funds', 'Investment Limit Reached', 'Verification Required', 'Technical Issues']}>
<Tab value="Wallet Insufficient Funds">

### Problem: Not Enough Funds in Wallet

If you encounter an insufficient funds error:

1. **Check your wallet balance** in the Wallet section
2. **Deposit additional funds** using supported payment methods
3. **Wait for deposit confirmation** (usually 5-30 minutes depending on method)
4. **Retry your investment** once funds are available

<Callout type="info">
  Always maintain a buffer amount in your wallet to cover any transaction fees or currency fluctuations.
</Callout>

</Tab>
<Tab value="Investment Limit Reached">

### Problem: Investment Amount Restrictions

Some startups have minimum or maximum investment limits:

- **Minimum Investment**: Review the startup's minimum threshold. Some opportunities require $100, while others may need $1,000+
- **Maximum Investment**: Check if there's a per-investor cap to ensure fair distribution among investors
- **Accredited Investor Status**: Some opportunities may require accredited investor verification

Contact support if you believe limits should not apply to your account.

</Tab>
<Tab value="Verification Required">

### Problem: Additional Verification Needed

If prompted for additional verification:

1. **Complete KYC verification** if not already done
2. **Provide accredited investor documentation** if required
3. **Submit proof of funds** for large investments
4. **Wait for approval** (typically 24-48 hours)

<Callout type="tip">
  Complete full verification early to avoid delays when you find the perfect investment opportunity.
</Callout>

</Tab>
<Tab value="Technical Issues">

### Problem: Investment Transaction Fails

If your investment transaction fails:

1. **Check your internet connection** stability
2. **Clear browser cache** and retry
3. **Try a different browser** or device
4. **Contact support** with transaction reference number

Your funds are safe and will remain in your wallet if a transaction fails.

</Tab>
</Tabs>
