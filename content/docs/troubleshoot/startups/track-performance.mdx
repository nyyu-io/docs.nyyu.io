## Common Questions

<Tabs items={['How often are valuations updated?', 'Can I export my data?', 'What if a startup stops updating?', 'Can I get tax documents?']}>
<Tab value="How often are valuations updated?">

### Valuation Update Frequency

Startup valuations are updated when:

- **New funding round**: Valuation adjusts based on the price per share in the new round
- **Annual review**: NYYU may adjust valuations based on performance and market conditions
- **Material events**: Major positive or negative developments may trigger valuation adjustments
- **M&A activity**: Acquisition offers or mergers will update your investment value

<Callout type="info">
  Note that startup valuations between funding rounds are estimates. True value is only realized at an exit event (acquisition, IPO, or sale of shares).
</Callout>

</Tab>
<Tab value="Can I export my data?">

### Data Export Options

Yes, you can export:

- **Portfolio summary**: PDF or CSV with all investments and current values
- **Individual investment details**: Complete history and documentation
- **Transaction history**: All deposits, investments, and withdrawals
- **Tax documents**: Year-end summaries for tax preparation
- **Performance reports**: Custom date ranges and metrics

All exports maintain your privacy and are securely delivered.

</Tab>
<Tab value="What if a startup stops updating?">

### Handling Communication Gaps

If a startup fails to provide timely updates:

1. **Platform notification**: We'll flag startups that miss reporting deadlines
2. **Direct outreach**: NYYU contacts the startup on behalf of investors
3. **Escalation**: Persistent silence triggers deeper investigation
4. **Investor protection**: Material issues are disclosed to all investors

You can also contact startups directly through the platform messaging system.

</Tab>
<Tab value="Can I get tax documents?">

### Tax Reporting & Documents

NYYU provides tax-related documents:

- **Annual investment summary**: For tax filing purposes
- **K-1 forms**: If applicable for your investments
- **Capital gains/losses**: When you sell or exit investments
- **Transaction records**: Complete history for audit trails

Consult with your tax advisor for specific guidance on reporting startup investments.

</Tab>
</Tabs>
