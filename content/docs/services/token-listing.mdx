# Token Listing on NYYU

Thank you for your interest in listing your token on NYYU, the innovative decentralized investment banking platform. To ensure a smooth and efficient listing process, please complete the application form provided below.

<Callout type="warn">
If any information is found to be false or invalid, your project will not be considered for listing on NYYU.
</Callout>

## Application Process

<Steps>
<Step>
### Submit Your Application
Fill out the [Token Listing Application Form](https://nyyu.io/token-listing-form) with accurate and detailed information about your project.
</Step>

<Step>
### Initial Review
Our Research Department will review your application. This includes evaluating the project's feasibility, compliance, and potential alignment with NYYU's mission and values.
</Step>

<Step>
### Feedback and Workflow
Upon passing the initial review, we will share the listing workflow with you, along with some useful tips to facilitate the listing process. An account manager will be assigned to guide you through each step.
</Step>

<Step>
### Account Manager Support
Your assigned account manager will reach out to you to discuss the next steps and provide continuous support throughout the listing process.
</Step>
</Steps>

## Evaluation Criteria

Our evaluation process considers several factors:

<Accordions>
<Accordion title="Innovation and Technology">
The uniqueness and technological advancements of your project.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Team and Background">
The experience and credibility of the team behind the project.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Market Potential">
The project's market potential, including demand and adoption metrics.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Compliance and Security">
Adherence to legal and regulatory standards, and robust security measures.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Community Engagement">
The level of engagement and support from the project's community.
</Accordion>
</Accordions>
